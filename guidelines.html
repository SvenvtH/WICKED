Main

Areas

Actions

  • - Recruits are NOT allowed to order anyone else around
  • - Recruits have to stay on their pads in the main room unless a Cadet or higher tells them to or when there is a raid
  • - Recruits are NOT allowed to check non-wckd for weapons or virus
  • - Recruits are NOT allowed to escort people to mines or to the waiting room
